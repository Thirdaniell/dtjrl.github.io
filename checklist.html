// After you enter — read this

You've done your analysis. The trade is placed. Your job is now over.

Do not watch every tick. Price does not move faster because you're watching it.

Your stop is placed for a reason. Do not move it closer out of fear, or further out of hope.

Your target is placed for a reason. Let price reach it. Trailing early is just fear in disguise.

If the trade stops you out — that's the cost of doing business. You followed the process. The process is what matters, not one trade.

If the trade wins — do not immediately look for the next one. You've done enough today.

Close the chart. Trust the work you did before the entry. That's where the real trading happened.
